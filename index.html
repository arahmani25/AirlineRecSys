Key Business Insight

Our analysis reveals that customer satisfaction is not uniform across passenger segments. Just like smartphone buyers (iPhone loyalists vs. price-sensitive consumers), airline passengers have distinct needs and expectations based on their travel class, purpose, and loyalty status.

A one-size-fits-all recommendation approach would fail to capture these nuances. Our system uses strategic segmentation to deliver personalized service improvement recommendations.
